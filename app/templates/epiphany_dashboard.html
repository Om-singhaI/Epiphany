The 5-step autonomous loop

A continuous cycle modelled on how a real data scientist works.

01 · Fivetran
Trigger

Wakes when fresh data syncs.

02 · Elastic
Explore

Discovers schema & real signals.

03 · Gemini
Reason

Forms a falsifiable hypothesis.

04 · Sandbox
Validate

Runs the right test (p<0.05).

05 · GitLab
Deploy

Trains a model & opens an MR.

Adaptive statistics

Chi-square, t-test, ANOVA or correlation — chosen automatically from your columns and run on real rows.

Real trained models

A scikit-learn model is trained and scored on held-out data, then saved as a loadable artifact.

Sandboxed & safe

Generated code is AST-screened and executed in a network-isolated, resource-limited sandbox.
